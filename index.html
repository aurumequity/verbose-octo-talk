The Engineer's Perspective

The host of Grace in the Glitch is a software engineer and sociologist currently working as an independent tech consultant. With a front-row seat to the rapid deployment of 2026's most disruptive technologies — from AI to bio-integration — they provide a blunt, research-driven analysis of the modern technological collapse.

This project is a "Digital Sanctuary" for those who recognize the glitch in the system and are looking for the ancient, un-trended Truth that remains when the signal drifts.

No personality cult. No algorithm-chasing. No affiliate partnerships. Just source code, scripture, and structured analysis — delivered without apology.
